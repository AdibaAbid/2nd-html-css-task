Beef Malai Boti

Beef when combines with spices, yougurt and lemon juice it makes a perfect treat for everyone.
Warm up the family and friends with scrumptious Beef Malai Boti.- Recipe by Zubaida Tariq.

Ingredients

  • Beef boti 1/2kg
  • Tikka boti masala 2tbsp
  • Ground ginger 1 tbsp
  • Red chili powder 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala powder 1 tbsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Raw papaya paste 2 tbsp
  • Ground green chilies 4
  • Yougurt 1 cup
  • Oil 1/2 cup
  • Lemon 2
  • Fresh cream 1 cup

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lightly flat beef boti with hammer.
  2. In a bowl put beef boti, tikka boti masala, ground ginger, red chili powder, raw papaya paste, ground green chilies, salt and yougurt. Marinate it for half an hour.
  3. Put beef mixture in a pan and cook on a slow flame until it turns into tender and water dries.
  4. Now add oil and cook till oil comes on top.
  5. Then add lemon juice and cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve delicious Malai boti with roghani naan.
